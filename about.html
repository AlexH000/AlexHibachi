Our History

Welcome to Hibachi Restaurant, where we offer a unique dining experience with our authentic Japanese hibachi cuisine. Our restaurant was established in 1995 by our founder, Chef Hiroshi, who has over 30 years of experience in preparing delicious hibachi meals.

Our mission is to provide our guests with a memorable dining experience that combines the art of hibachi cooking with the warmth of Japanese hospitality. We take pride in using the freshest ingredients, carefully selected from local markets, to create mouthwatering dishes that are cooked to perfection right at your table by our talented and entertaining hibachi chefs.

Over the years, we have become a beloved destination for families, friends, and special occasions. We are dedicated to delivering exceptional service and a delightful dining experience that leaves a lasting impression on our valued guests.
